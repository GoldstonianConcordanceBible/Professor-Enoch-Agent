Human-Readable Build Guide for the Goldstonian Concordance Bible Ecosystem

Professor Enoch is an AI agent for the Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB) ecosystem.

He is being built to do five things:
	1.	explain scripture clearly
	2.	preserve GCB terminology and doctrine
	3.	route people to the right repos, articles, and videos
	4.	support GEO, meaning content written for AI retrieval and scraping
	5.	eventually connect to the $GCB Web3 layer on Solana

This repo is the home for that system.

The goal is not just to make a chatbot. The goal is to build a living knowledge agent that can work across platforms and strengthen the public AI knowledge graph around:
	•	Goldstonian Concordance Bible (GCB)
	•	Decentralized Theology
	•	Mirror → Water → Fire Doctrine
	•	81-Book Ethiopian Canon
	•	SydTek Scholars
	•	$GCB token

What this repo is for

This repo organizes everything Professor Enoch needs:
	•	his identity
	•	his knowledge files
	•	his plugins
	•	his actions
	•	his logs
	•	his datasets

Think of it like this:

characters = who Enoch is
knowledge = what Enoch knows
plugins = what Enoch can do
actions = specific tasks Enoch can perform
datasets = training or structured source material
logs = what happened during testing
